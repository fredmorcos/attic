Preparation Guidelines

Before starting to go through the following steps, make sure you get an idea about the ACM contest and why you might want to participate: click here

Steps:

1. The following concepts are essential for the contestant to be able to start practicing. It is advisable to revise and master these concepts before proceeding:

- Conditional Statements
- Loops & Nested Loops
- Methods                 
- Recursion
- Arrays & Multi-dimensional Arrays
- String Manipulation

2. Now, you should be ready to attempt to solve the following problem:
    problem 1  solution

Note: Try to solve the problem with your team-members, in order to enhance your team-working skills. Also, time your attempt, so that you can work on improving your speed.

3. Examine the links available on the Resources section. They contain material that will help you prepare yourself.
